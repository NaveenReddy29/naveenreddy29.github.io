01 // About

Who I Am

I'm an AI/ML Engineer with 6+ years of experience based in San Jose, CA, building production LLM applications, RAG systems, and agentic AI across financial services, e-commerce, and consulting. M.S. in Big Data Analytics from San Diego State University (2024).

I specialize in the full LLM stack — from fine-tuning open-source models (QLoRA/PEFT on Llama, Mistral) and building multi-agent orchestration with LangGraph and CrewAI, to designing RAG pipelines with vector databases (Pinecone, Weaviate, ChromaDB) and deploying on AWS EKS with vLLM.

I thrive at the intersection of research and engineering — taking cutting-edge architectures and making them reliable, observable, and production-ready with LangSmith, Arize AI, Guardrails AI, and Kubernetes.
